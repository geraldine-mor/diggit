Diggit Community Guidelines

Welcome to Diggit. This is a space for sharing ideas, discussion, and community. By creating an account, you agree to follow these guidelines.

1. Be Respectful

You agree to engage respectfully with others at all times.

  • No offensive, abusive, or hateful language
  • No harassment, threats, or intimidation
  • No deliberately provocative or disruptive behaviour

2. No Harmful or Inappropriate Content

  • No obscene or offensive content
  • No misleading or defamatory content
  • No illegal content or promotion of illegal activity

3. No External Links

To help keep Diggit safe and free from spam, external links are not permitted at this time.

  • No posting links in content, comments, or profiles

4. Account Responsibility

  • You are responsible for your account and its activity
  • You must keep your login details secure

5. Enforcement

We may take action if these guidelines are violated, including:

  • Removing content
  • Suspending or banning accounts

6. Updates

These guidelines may change over time. Continued use of Diggit means you accept any updates.

7. Agreement

By signing up, you confirm that you have read and agree to these guidelines.
